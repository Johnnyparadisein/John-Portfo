About Me

Hello there! I'm John, a passionate Graphic Designer and AI Musketeer based in Tbilisi, Georgia. I thrive on crafting visually striking designs where aesthetics meet innovation and cutting-edge technology.

My expertise spans Graphic Design, UI/UX, Social Media, and exploring the frontiers of AI in the creative process.
